Ukiyoe Art Collection

I have a collection of Ukiyoe prints that were created during the late Edo period, also known as the woodblock printing era in Japan's latter half of the 1800s. Although it is common to observe some color fading in the original prints, it is worth noting that all colors utilized in that era were sourced from natural ingredients, lending them an overall soft and gentle appearance. It was during this time that European artists began studying the Ukiyoe art style, and notable artists such as Vincent Van Gogh's painting style show great influence from Ukiyoe art.
